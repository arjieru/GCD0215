This file describes the variables, the data, and any transformations or work that was performed to clean up the data.

A full description of the data is available at the site where it was obtained: 
http://archive.ics.uci.edu/ml/datasets/Human+Activity+Recognition+Using+Smartphones 

Data source: 
https://d396qusza40orc.cloudfront.net/getdata%2Fprojectfiles%2FUCI%20HAR%20Dataset.zip 

Variables:

x_train, y_train, subject_train, x_test, y_test, subject_test are all used to read and cobtain the raw data from the assignment.

x1 is the concatenation of all X data.
y1 is the concatenation of all Y data.
subject1 is the concatenation of all Subject data.

features contains features.txt

mean_std - contains the subsets of the mean and standard deviation
activity_labels contains activity_labels.txt
final is the merging of all variables.
final1 is the cleand up version of final.

final_data.txt is the file with cleaned up data(final1).
